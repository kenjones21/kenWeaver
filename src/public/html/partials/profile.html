About Me

Welcome to my website! As you've probably guessed, my name is Ken Weaver. I'm currently finishing up my Masters degree in Physical Chemistry at University of Pennsylvania. I'm passionate about climate change, computer science, and politics. I believe that, in more ways than one, we are at a point in history where the world will change very quickly. I am interested in helping to make sure that, as a society, we are prepared as best as we can be for those changes. Speaking broadly, I believe that the best way to do that is with robust, open systems and institutions that are subject to feedback from the public and from experts. For technology, I think this means open source development when it serves the public interest.

My skills include knowledge of several programming languages, including Python and C++. I am familiar with most common algorithms and data structures, and can leverage ideas from these to create application specific instances of both. I have a small amount of web design experience, largely back end, although I can design the full stack when necessary. My familiarity with technologies includes Node, Angular, Express, and MongoDB, which are used for this site, as well as Matplotlib and Git.
