Safety

Okay, so please don't be stupid. I don't want anyone to get hurt (and neither do you, hopefully), so be smart about lab practices.

As far as attire goes, make sure you wear pants (not leggings), close toed shoes (cover your whole foot), and shirts with sleeves. Make sure you ALWAYS have eye protection, as well as your lab aprons and gloves. Any questions, email me.
